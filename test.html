About the Conference

The National Conference on Computational Intelligence and Applications (NC2IA) brings together researchers, practitioners and academicians to discuss advancements, challenges, and future directions in the field of computational intelligence. This event aims to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing among professionals from various domains of artificial intelligence and machine learning.

In addition to oral presentations and an innovation exhibition, this event will provide an opportunity for researchers and students to showcase their innovative products. The primary objectives include knowledge exchange, research advancement, networking, and publication opportunities.

Conference Topics

  • Artificial Intelligence and ML
  • Fuzzy Systems and Neural Networks
  • Data Science, Big Data, Cloud Computing
  • Bioinformatics, Biomedical engineering
  • Computer Graphics & Virtual Reality
  • Optimization and Computational Intelligence
  • Database & Information Systems
  • Forensic data analysis
  • Robotics and Data Analytics
